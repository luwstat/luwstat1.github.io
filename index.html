I am currently a tenure-track Assistant Professor of Statistics in Department of Mathematics at Western New England University. I received my PhD in Statistics at the University of South Carolina. I graduated with a Bachelor of Medicine in West China School of Medicine from Sichuan University.

My research interests include Semi-Parametric Modeling, Bayesian Modeling and Computing, Complex Censored Data, Panel Count Data, Invertible Neural Network, Reinforcement Learning, Survival Analysis, Mixture Model, Longitudinal Data, Joint Modeling, Convex Optimization..
